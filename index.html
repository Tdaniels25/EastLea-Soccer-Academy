ABOUT US EastLea Academy

Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive environment that promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and personal growth.

At EastLea Soccer Academy, we are passionate about empowering children through soccer. Our dedicated coach has [X] years of experience and a strong commitment to providing free coaching to small kids in our community.

We train basic soccer techinques and general life skills to youths as young as 3yrs up to the age of 20 years. We address social vices such as HIV & AIDS, child abuse, drug & alcohol abuse, teenage pregnancies,christian values and general behaviour.

We offer free coaching to small kids in our community, keeping them off the streets and engaged in a healthy activity. By supporting this cause, you are helping provide a nurturing environment for children, promoting physical fitness, and fostering social cohesion within our community.

FAQ

Question that are frequently asked questions

How can I try out for the team?

We hold tryouts at the beginning of each season. Stay updated on our website or social media platforms for tryout announcements and registration details.

What are the age requirements to join the team?

Our team is open to players of various age groups. Please refer to the specific age divisions mentioned in our tryout announcements or contact us for more information.

When does the season start and end?

The season usually starts in [month] and ends in [month]. However, exact dates may vary depending on the league and competition schedules. We will provide a detailed season calendar to all team members.

How often are practices held?

We typically have practice sessions [number of times] per week. The specific practice schedule will be communicated by the coach at the beginning of the season.

Where are practices and games held?

Practices are held at [location], while games are played at various venues depending on the league and competition fixtures. Detailed information about practice and game locations will be provided to team members.

Are there any team fees or membership costs?

Yes, there are usually team fees to cover expenses such as uniforms, equipment, league registrations, and facility rentals. The specific fees and payment details will be communicated to players and their families.

Can parents or guardians attend practices and games?

Parents and guardians are welcome to attend practices and games to support the team. However, we request that they follow any guidelines or restrictions set by the coaching staff or league regarding spectator participation.

What equipment do I need to bring?

Players are required to bring appropriate soccer cleats, shin guards, and a water bottle to every practice and game. Additionally, it is recommended to have suitable clothing for various weather conditions.

Will transportation be provided for away games?

Generally, transportation to away games is the responsibility of the players and their families. Carpooling with other team members is encouraged to ensure everyone can attend the games.

Can I play for another team while being a member of this team?

We expect our players to prioritize their commitment to our team. Playing for multiple teams simultaneously may conflict with training and game schedules. Any conflicts should be discussed with the coach beforehand.

How can I communicate with the coaching staff or team management?

We have designated communication channels, such as team meetings, email, or instant messaging groups, to facilitate communication between players, parents, and the coaching staff. Details about these channels will be provided to team members.

Are there any volunteer opportunities for parents?

Yes, we appreciate parental involvement and often have volunteer opportunities for various team-related tasks, such as organizing events, fundraising, or coordinating team logistics. Please reach out to our team management if you are interested in volunteering.
