Occasionally funds permitting we go out in the rural areas with outreach programs to empower youths through coaching clinics and some small donations of footballs and other training equipment.

Our commitment to the community goes beyond the field. We take pride in our impactful outreach programs that aim to make a positive difference in the lives of those around us. Our Community Outreach Initiative is at the heart of our academy's values. We believe that soccer has the power to unite and inspire, and through our programs, we aim to extend these benefits to underserved and deserving members of our community.

Fitness and Wellness

Our Fitness and Wellness programs were motivated by COVID 19 when people became confined in their homes because of lockdown during Covid era. Obesity and other related complications such as high Bp, diabetes kicked in due to lack of exercising. Coach Jojo initiated a small local fitness and wellness club where we would go do football related exercises helping members to sweat it out. In no time our training programs grew in leaps and bounds attracting all and sundry in different shapes, ages and sizes.

Zimbabwe Olympic Committee

Local teachers are trained through workshops on Olympic and Futbolnet values which help us to shun and do away with violence in our society. Trained teachers will then go and train their kids whom they later bring to Olympic Centre for a festival and tournament to choose the best kids to display and demonstrate life values and life skills during competitions.

Coaching Programs

We train Saturdays morning during school term to avoid clashing with school curriculums since most of our players are of school going age. However we provide small group sessions and/or even individual sessions per order.

We concentrate on basic soccer techniques such as:
Ball mastery encompassing various kicks, tactical learning on formations and transitions, psychological growth involving mental toughness and fair play, education on diet and health, and instilling a culture of respect towards the body, environment, and others. Through these elements, the academy aims to nurture not only soccer skills but also holistic personal growth in young players.

In essence, our programs at Eastlea Soccer Academy comprehensively cover all facets of football development. This includes refining technical skills encompassing various techniques, enhancing players' physical conditioning, fostering mental resilience, and emphasizing the significance of proper nutrition as a means of effectively fueling the body – the primary instrument of their trade.
